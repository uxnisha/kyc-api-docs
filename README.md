## Orientation Day 1 — Agile & Scrum
**Date:** Tue Feb 24 2026
**Topic:** How Modern Software Gets Built

### What I learned today

**Traditional vs Agile**
- Traditional (Waterfall/CPMCD) is linear — 
  requirements locked upfront, changes are expensive
- Agile starts with core requirements only, 
  ships incrementally, collects feedback, loops

**The Scrum Framework**
- Product Owner (PO) maintains the Product Backlog 
  (PB) — a living prioritised list
- Sprint Planning selects items into Sprint Backlog
- Sprint = time-boxed iteration, 2–4 weeks
- Daily Scrum (standup) = 15 minutes every day
- Each sprint produces a Potentially Shippable 
  Product Increment (PSPI)
- Sprint Review = show the product to stakeholders
- Sprint Retrospective = team reflects on its 
  own process and improves it
- Then the loop repeats
